About Us

Situmbeko Artspace celebrates Botswana’s artistic spirit, blending heritage with modern creativity. Based in Kasane, our mission is to share the beauty of our land, the Chobe River, and the diamond-rich culture with an international audience. Every piece tells a story, a whisper of Botswana’s heart.
